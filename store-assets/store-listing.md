# Play Store Listing — Noise Machine

## Short Description (80 chars max)
Sleep noise generator with one Color slider. White to brown, shaped by you.

## Full Description (4000 chars max)

Noise Machine generates real-time ambient noise to mask distractions and help you sleep. One Color slider shapes the tone from bright white noise through balanced pink to deep brown — no presets, no menus, just a smooth continuum you can tune to your preference.

Designed for bedside use in the dark when you're tired. The interface is minimal and calm: a play button, a color slider, and a sleep timer. That's it.

FEATURES

• Color slider — continuously shapes the noise from bright and airy (white) through balanced (pink) to deep and soft (brown)
• Sleep timer — auto-stop with smooth fade-out (15 min, 30 min, 1 hour, 2 hours)
• Background playback — keeps playing with the screen off, all night if you need it
• Smooth fades — configurable fade-in and fade-out so playback starts and stops gently
• Texture control — adjust from smooth to grainy for a different feel
• Stereo width — from mono to gently decorrelated stereo
• Micro drift — subtle slow tonal wandering for a more organic, less static sound
• Dark interface — easy on the eyes at night

WHAT MAKES IT DIFFERENT

• One slider, not a wall of presets. The Color control smoothly sweeps through the entire noise spectrum.
• Real-time synthesis. All audio is generated live on your device — no looping audio files, no repeating patterns.
• No volume slider. Use your phone's hardware volume. One less thing to fiddle with.
• No ads, no tracking, no internet. The app has zero network access. Your settings stay on your device.

TECHNICAL DETAILS

Audio is generated by a real-time DSP pipeline: white noise source → spectral shaping (cascaded biquad filters) → texture processing → gain safety → stereo decorrelation → AudioTrack output. All parameter changes go through exponential smoothers for artifact-free transitions. The render loop is allocation-free for glitch-free multi-hour playback.

PERMISSIONS

• Foreground Service — keeps audio playing when the screen is off
• Notifications — shows a stop button in the notification shade (playback works even if denied)

No internet permission. No microphone. No location. No contacts. No storage.
